**Project Spec
A SIMPLE software (MVP) with the following features:
- users register with specified roles (admin can add users with roles)
- patients upload their BioData & medical records
- patients report their adverse events
- clinicians get notifications
- clinicians view clients' BioData & medical records
- clinicians reply the patients via messaging (consultation)
- clinicians make prescriptions
- patients view prescriptions


#####################
**User story
Features/user story/workflow
1. Patient UPDATES profile/BioData
2. Patient UPLOADS medical records: diagnosis, treatment, lab reports
------------pay------------ 80/20
3. Patient SUBMITS reports of AE
4. Clinician GETS notification about the reports
5. Clinician VIEWS patients BioData & medical records
6. Clinician REPLIES patients via messaging (consultation)
7. Clinician ORDERS PRESCRIPTIONS
8. Patient VIEWS the prescriptions


Real time features
- Clinician schedules APPOINTMENT with the client
- Send notifications to both patients and clinicians for appointment
- Clinician counsels the client via chat/audio CONSULTATION


######################
**Benefits over WhatsApp
- ePRO not chat app
- remote healthcare
- stores & displays medical records
- useful for research purposes
